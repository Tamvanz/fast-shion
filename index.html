Misi



Minimizing the Effect of Fast Fashion (MISSION 4.0)

Changing Habit

change the consumptive lifestyle and care more about the social, environmental and economic conditions of the world.

Suggestion

reduce the number of purchases by switching to more expensive items, but have the quality to last longer or shop at used clothing.

Champaigne

through a movement aimed at the entire community, especially the younger generation who love Fast Fashion products but do not know the effects and consequences of their own consumptive behavior, this movement will be held using champaigne with hastag #Istopfastfashion

Motivating

Invite people to switch to products that sustain "slow fashion", namely fashion brands that pay attention to the impact of fashion production on industrial workers, consumers, and ecosystems.

Participating

Donating clothes that have already been purchased and not used anymore to those who need them.
